HOOTANANNY

For 19 years, Hootananny has been promoting the best grass roots music in Scotland. Mumford and Sons, Sam Fender, Jake Bugg, Dougie Mclean and Skerryvore have all played the venue.

The building is a lovely traditional Victorian Inverness old sweety shop with traditional Scottish music on our ground floor and indie, hiphop,ska and rock upstairs on the Madhatters stage.

The family run business has been awarded Scotland’s Best Live Music Venue 2019 by Scottish Bar and Pub Awards and Best Inverness Pub of the Year, 8 years running, by Inverness City Advertiser. Open daily for pub drinkers, we have a delicious Scottish menu of traditional dishes cooked to perfection from 6 pm.

FIG & THISTLE

Fig & Thistle Bistro offers modern Scottish cuisine in a casual dining venue in the heart of the city centre, Using Quality local produce with daily specials, they offer value for money and a warm welcome.

This is a great place for a date night, or a special occasion. The staff has received a great amount of praise for their service and their inclination to go the extra mile for their guests. Enjoy an array of traditional Scottish food with a gourmet twist at this local, family run, neighbourhood bistro.

STACKS

Come and enjoy Stacks deli, bakery, and coffee house. All of Stack’s food is made fresh, to order and in-house with lots of creative licence! Comfort food takes time, but it's worth the wait! Menus are seasonal to keep the very best on the plate all year round.

Food waste is a major problem in Scotland & Stacks boasts an impressive rate of less than 5%, reducing their impact on the big rock we call home.

Stacks was created to offer a warm space for everything from business meetings, a celebration dinner or just a cup of coffee with your four-legged friend!
