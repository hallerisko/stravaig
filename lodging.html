HOTELS

Nestled on the leafy banks of the River Ness, lies Ness Walk. An idyllic 5-star retreat hotel in Inverness, just 10 minutes from Loch Ness.

Surrounded by a dramatic canopy of impressive trees, our grand building allows you to experience a secluded atmosphere in the heart of the vibrant city centre. Originally a 19th century house, our lovingly restored Grade B listed building still retains many of its periodic features. But unified with modern design and architecture, providing a flawless balance between past and present.

Ness Walk is also home to the Torrish Restaurant. Presenting the perfect combination of innovation and intimacy, Torrish breathes new life to fine dining in Inverness.

AIRBNB

Built in 1920, Seacot Cottage is a comfortable, traditional cottage in the former fishing village of Clachnaharry beside the Caledonian Canal in Inverness.

The holiday home has been tastefully modernised whilst retaining many of the period features. It includes two bedrooms, four beds, and two baths. It is suitable for up to six guests, and is a self check-in property.

There is a period Iron column radiator in the dining room to keep warm and an electric fireplace in the TV room. There are 3 other original fireplaces throughout the cottage. Although these are not active, they add to the character of the house.
